## Business Questions

This analysis aims to answer the following operational questions:

1. How has demand changed over time, and does it affect operational performance?
   
   **Why it matters:**
   1. Helps Operations anticipate workload spikes.
   2. Determines whether delays are driven by capacity constraints.

   **KPIs:**

   1. Monthly orders
   2. Order growth rate
   3. Delivery delay rate by month
   4. Average fulfillment time by month
   
2. Which stages of the order fulfillment process contribute most to delivery delays?

   **Why it matters:**
   1. Directly identifies operational bottlenecks.
   2. Provides actionable recommendations.

   **KPIs:**
   1. Processing time
   2. Seller fulfillment time
   3. Shipping time
   4. Total delivery time
   5. On-time delivery rate

3. How do delivery delays impact customer satisfaction?

   **Why it matters:**
   1. Connects operational performance to customer outcomes.
   2. Justifies investment in operational improvements.

   **KPIs:**
   1. Review score
   2. Delay days
   3. Percentage of low-rated orders
   4. Customer satisfaction by delivery status

## KPI definitions
   1. Total Orders = Number of unique orders.
   2. Revenue = Total sales value.
   3. Average delivery time = Average number of days between purchase and delivery.
